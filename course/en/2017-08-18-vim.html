Vi IMproved’s Learning Session

VIM’s help file is the main source of knowledge about VIM and his functionalities. This newsletter let you definitely learn this awesome text-editor throughout daily or weekly email. Each email has a theory section and a practice one. Even if you are an advance VIM user, I am sure you will find useful exercises, tips and you will improve your knowledge. Join us end enjoy your new way of learning!

Subscribers receive emails with:

  • Theory: a portion of the official manual (help file);
  • Practice: questions to enhance memorization and improve the learning process.
  • Help: answers to questions.

See an email preview!

What motivate me to create this service:

  • avoid users from reinventing the wheel. Write plugins for functionalities already covered in the core software is time wasted;
  • spread helpful knowledge to every user from beginner to advanced;
  • support Vim;
  • You definitely MUST try it! I know.
