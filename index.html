Vimly is a new way of learning and reading the official Vim Manual. There are no ads, no third-party tracking scripts and it is not required a login.

Motivation: There are so much awesomeness behind VIM that you could not even imagine. Before install thousand of plugin, patch your vimrc and searching on the web about how to start (or close), READ THE MANUAL.

I know, laziness is on the corner, a whole manual???

Here vimly comes in handy! Read it one chunk at a time.

We must remember what ancients have always said:

A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.

Excerpt from the text Tao Te Ching.

Note: There is also a practice section. Deliberate practice has been scientifically proven in order to be an expert!. Answer the questions and do exercises after reading the theory, helps you to learn better and strength your memories.
