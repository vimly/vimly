Vimly is a new way of learning and reading the official Vim Manual. There are no ads, no third-party tracking scripts and it is not required a login. You are ready to get full content only with your email.

Motivation: We all love official manuals and it is needless to say that each expert must RTFM. However, too often, we are too lazy to read an entire manual from the beginning to the end. There is a solution to this problem! Read it one chunk at a time. Reading a bit of it in the bathroom, in a queue, before sleeping and each time we have a small slot of time.

We must remember what ancients have always said:

A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.
Excerpt from the text Tao Te Ching.

Note: If you think you are not lazy or if you have already read the manual, do not despair! Join as well and improve your knowledge throughout the practice section. Deliberate practice has been scientifically proven in order to be an expert!. Try to answer the questions and reading the theory one more time, help you consolidate your memories.
